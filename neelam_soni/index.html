Plans & Pricing explained

Here’s what you need to know about Pepipost Plans & Pricing, based on the questions we get asked the most.

Do you offer a free trial?

All new accounts start off on our full-featured free plan with 30,000 emails per month. It’s free forever, till you wish to upgrade. We don’t even ask for credit card.

Can I buy dedicated IPs?

Dedicated IPs are available at a cost of $20 per month. To add dedicated IPs to your account, contact us at support@pepipost.com

Why should I follow warm-up plan?

Irrespective of the plan in which you are, it's important to scale your emails gradually to maintain your sender domain reputation and deliverability. At Pepipost, we look at sending emails for new prospects in a small chunk with a warm up process spread across 2 weeks. Know More

How is cashback on all emails opened calculated?

Here’s how it works. At the end of each billing cycle, we calculate the total emails opened for the month and discount the cashback amount in your invoice. The per email cashback rate depends on the plan you are in. For instance, if you are in the 500,000 monthly email plan, your per email cost is $0.0029. You earn cashback at the same rate ($0.0029 per email). Find out about cashback in the event plan upgrade or downgrade here

What happens if I go over my plan’s monthly email limit?

Be rest assured, crossing the monthly email limit will not block you from sending emails. Extra emails on monthly plans are calculated at the end of your billing cycle and included in the invoice for the upcoming month. This will reflect as Overage in your invoice.

Do unused emails roll over into the next month?

No. If you are under your allocated monthly email sending limit at the end of a billing period, the unused email credits do not roll over to the next billing period.

What payment types do you accept?

We accept payments via all major credit/ debit cards, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Diners, and JCB. We do not accept PayPal.

Do you offer high-volume plans?

Yes! Drop an email to sales@pepipost.com and tell us about your requirement and the pricing you're looking for.
